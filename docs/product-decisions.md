# Product Decisions

## Navigation

Workout is not a persistent tab. It is a global action because it is task-oriented and needs faster access than a browse destination.

## Local-first stance

The initial base intentionally avoids backend dependence. The first promise of the app is reliability on-device.

## Visual identity

The app uses a blue-frog inspired palette and explicitly avoids green, including positive states and charts.

## Scope discipline

Anything social, cloud-based or not fully verified by the research stays out of the critical path and can be added later via feature flags or dedicated phases.

## Manual-only tracking

This product version is intentionally manual-only. No wearable, Health Connect, Apple Health or similar ingestion is planned in the current roadmap.
